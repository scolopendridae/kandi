About


Hello, I am a student who makes the beaded bracelets (and more) typically known as kandi as a hobby. I would like to set up a shop at some point, but for now this site serves as an archive for what I've created (a lot of which I have since traded away!). Feel free to use this site as inspiration for your own works.
